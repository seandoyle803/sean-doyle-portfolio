About Me

I'm a Business Analytics and Information Systems student at the University of Iowa, graduating in May 2027. I work with SQL, Python, Power BI, and Excel to pull meaning from messy data and translate it into decisions that actually matter.

My work is project-driven — I'm most effective when I can go from a raw dataset to a clear finding. Whether that means building a relational database, running regression models, or integrating hundreds of rows of statistics into a single analytical view, I follow the work wherever it leads.

I'm looking to bring that approach to a team that values analytical thinking, clear communication, and getting things done.
