# Product Requirement Document

## 01 — Problem Statement

Sean Doyle is a Business Analytics and Information Systems student at the University of Iowa, graduating in May 2027. He is seeking Data Analyst Intern and Business Analyst Intern roles across a variety of industries.

A traditional resume lists skills and experiences but does not effectively demonstrate Sean’s ability to apply those skills in real-world scenarios. This landing page solves that problem by showcasing tangible projects, tools, and outcomes, allowing recruiters and hiring managers to see proof of his analytical capabilities. The goal is to position Sean as a candidate who not only understands data tools, but can use them to generate meaningful insights and complete projects end-to-end.

## 02 — Target Audience

Primary audience:
- Recruiters and hiring managers hiring for Data Analyst Intern and Business Analyst Intern roles

Audience characteristics:
- Work at large companies or startups
- Hiring for entry-level analytics roles
- Looking for candidates with applied project experience
- Interested in candidates who can demonstrate problem-solving using data

What they are looking for:
- Clear evidence of completed analytics projects
- Demonstrated ability to use tools like SQL, Python, Excel, and Power BI
- Proof of extracting insights from data, not just technical execution
- Signals of drive, reliability, and ability to follow through on work

## 03 — Goals & Success Metrics

| Goal | How We Measure It |
|------|------------------|
| Drive traffic to the site | ~10 visitors per month |
| Generate recruiter interest | At least 1 recruiter outreach within 30–60 days |
| Support job applications | Used in 10+ applications |
| Improve interview performance | Referenced in interviews as proof of work |
